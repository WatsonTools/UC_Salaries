How far behind national wage growth has your academic salary fallen?

Pick the year you started and your academic position. The calculator compares the actual salary for that role against a counterfactual where the same starting academic salary had kept pace with average wage growth in the private sector.

The calculation uses the actual salary for your selected position in the chosen start year as the baseline.

The counterfactual scenario computes the average wage growth over time from the same starting salary. Neither scenario considers career advancement and progression to higher salary bands or promotions.
